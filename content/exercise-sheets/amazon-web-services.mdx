2. Create a billing alarm to keep track of your AWS bill.

   <Solution>

   1. `CloudWatch > Alarms > Billing > Create alarm`.
   2. `Select metric > Metrics > Billing > Total Estimated Charge`, then select the `EstimatedCharges` metric.
   3. **`than...`**: `5 USD`.
   4. `Create new topic`.
   5. **`Create a new topic...`**: `Billing_Alerts`.
   6. **`Email endpoints that will receive the notification...`**: `salmani.soheil@gmail.com`.
   7. `Create topic`.
   8. **`Alarm name`**: `Billing_Alert`.
   9. **`Alarm description`**: "Alert me through email whenever usage goes above 5$.".

   </Solution>
