1. What are the four panels in Media Encoder?

   <Solution>

   - **Media Browser:** it lets you browse media of various formats on your
     system.
   - **Queue:** it is the heart of the application. Here you find the list of
     the encoding jobs.
   - **Preset Browser:** here you can find about any imaginable format to output
     your file.
   - **Encoding:** it monitors the progress of the current encoding job.

   </Solution>
